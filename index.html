Japan Travel Itinerary | April 18 - 20

April 18: Tokyo Exploration

Arrival at Sequence Iyashita Hotel - 17:00

Activities:

  • Meiji Shrine - 17:30–19:00 (A peaceful shrine nestled in nature.)
  • Yoyogi Park - 19:00–19:30 (Relax with a stroll in this spacious park.)
  • Asakusa Sensoji Temple - 19:45–20:30 (Explore one of Tokyo's oldest temples.)
  • Takeshita Street - 21:45–22:30 (A vibrant street filled with shops and food stalls.)

April 19: Mt. Fuji & Oshino Hakkai Adventure

Departure: 07:15 AM from Shinjuku

Activities:

  • Fruit Picking & Flower Viewing - 08:30–10:00 (Enjoy picking fruits and viewing seasonal flowers.)
  • Mt. Fuji 5th Station - 10:30–12:00 (Stunning views from the 5th station of Mt. Fuji.)
  • Kachi-Kachi Ropeway - 12:30–13:00 (A scenic ride offering breathtaking views.)
  • Oshino Hakkai - 14:30–16:00 (Natural springs with picturesque views of Mt. Fuji.)

April 20: Mt. Fuji & Hakone Day Tour

Departure: 08:00 AM from Shinjuku

Activities:

  • Hakone Shrine & Sea Torii Gate - 10:30–12:00 (A beautiful shrine with views of Mt. Fuji.)
  • Hakone Pirate Ship - 12:10–12:40 (Cruise on Lake Ashi with stunning views.)
  • Owakudani - 13:10–14:00 (Experience the volcanic atmosphere and enjoy Mt. Fuji views.)
  • Lake Yamanaka - 15:00–15:30 (A perfect spot for photos with swans and Mt. Fuji.)
  • Oshino Hakkai - 15:40–16:20 (Iconic natural springs at the base of Mt. Fuji.)
