Japan Travel Itinerary

April 17 - Flight from Delhi to Haneda Airport

  • 🛫 Depart from Indira Gandhi International Airport (Delhi)
  • 🕐 Evening arrival at Haneda Airport, Tokyo

April 18 - Tokyo: Asakusa, Sensoji, Ueno, Shibuya

  • 🛕 Visit Sensoji Temple – Tokyo’s oldest temple in Asakusa
  • 🌳 Explore Ueno Park and its museums
  • 📸 Shibuya Crossing and Hachiko Statue

April 19 - Day trip to Mount Fuji and Hakone

  • 🚌 Travel to Mount Fuji
  • 🚠 Ride the Hakone Ropeway
  • 🚤 Cruise on Lake Ashi

April 20 - Tokyo Disney Resort

  • 🎢 Full-day fun at Tokyo Disneyland or DisneySea

April 21 - TeamLab Planets and Akihabara

  • 🎨 Explore TeamLab Planets digital art museum
  • 🎮 Visit Akihabara for electronics and anime shopping

April 22 - Travel to Kyoto via Shinkansen

  • 🚅 Ride the bullet train from Tokyo to Kyoto
  • 🏯 Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine in the evening

April 23 - Kyoto: Arashiyama & Kinkaku-ji

  • 🌲 Walk through Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • 🌸 Explore Tenryu-ji Temple and Togetsukyo Bridge
  • 🏯 Visit the Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji)

April 24 - Nara Day Trip

  • 🦌 Feed deer in Nara Park
  • ⛩ Visit Todai-ji Temple with Japan’s largest bronze Buddha

April 25 - Kyoto Gion & Philosopher’s Path

  • 👘 Explore the Gion district (Geisha area)
  • 🌸 Walk along the Philosopher’s Path

April 26 - Kyoto to Osaka, Check-in at W Osaka

  • 🚅 Train to Osaka
  • 🏨 Check-in to W Osaka Hotel

April 27 - Hiroshima Day Trip

  • 🕊 Visit Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park and Museum
  • 🏯 Explore Hiroshima Castle and Shukkeien Garden
  • ⛩ Take ferry to Miyajima Island, visit Itsukushima Shrine

April 28 - Return to Osaka

  • 🚄 Train from Kyoto to Shin-Osaka (6:55 AM - 7:09 AM)
  • 🏨 Continue stay at W Osaka

April 29 - Osaka: Universal Studios, Castle & Dotonbori

  • 🏯 Visit Osaka Castle
  • 🎢 Enjoy Universal Studios Japan
  • 🍜 Explore Dotonbori Market for street food & lights

April 30 - Return to Delhi via Hong Kong

  • 🛫 Flight from Kansai Airport to Hong Kong
  • ⏳ 2-hour layover
  • 🛬 Flight to Delhi
