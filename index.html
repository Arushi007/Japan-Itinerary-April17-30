Japan Itinerary: April 18-19

Explore Tokyo, Mt. Fuji & More with Colorful Adventures!

April 18: Tokyo Exploration

Arrival: 17:00 at Sequence Iyashita Hotel

Activities: Visit Meiji Shrine, Yoyogi Park, Asakusa Sensoji Temple, Tokyo Tower, and Takeshita Street.

Start your adventure with a serene visit to Meiji Shrine, explore Yoyogi Park, marvel at Asakusa Sensoji Temple, take in the iconic Tokyo Tower views, and shop on the lively Takeshita Street!

Evening: Shibuya Crossing & Ginza

Explore the iconic Shibuya Crossing, try street food, and shop in the upscale Ginza district.

End your day in the vibrant Shibuya Crossing, savor delicious Japanese food from street vendors, and then head to Ginza for an evening of chic shopping and vibrant nightlife.

April 19: Mt. Fuji & Oshino Hakkai Adventure

Departure from Tokyo: 07:15 AM from Shinjuku Center Building

Activities: Seasonal fruit picking, flower viewing at various parks, Mt. Fuji 5th Station, Kachi-Kachi Ropeway, Oshino Hakkai.

Head to Yamanashi for fruit picking, breathtaking views of Mt. Fuji, and a delicious lunch at the 5th Station. Experience the scenic beauty with the Kachi-Kachi Ropeway and visit Oshino Hakkai for stunning views of the iconic mountain.

Oshino Hakkai & Return to Tokyo

Explore the scenic Oshino Hakkai, then return to Tokyo for the evening.
