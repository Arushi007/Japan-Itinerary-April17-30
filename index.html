Japan Travel Itinerary

April 17 - Flight from Delhi to Haneda

  • Depart from Delhi to Haneda Airport
  • Settle into Tokyo hotel

April 18 - Tokyo Sightseeing

  • Asakusa & Sensoji Temple – One of Tokyo’s most iconic temples
  • Ueno Park – Cherry blossoms, museums, and zoo
  • Shibuya Crossing – The world’s busiest pedestrian crossing

April 19 - Mount Fuji Day Trip

  • Visit Mount Fuji 5th Station
  • Lake Kawaguchi – Picturesque views of Mt. Fuji

April 20 - Travel to Kyoto

  • Take the Shinkansen (bullet train) to Kyoto
  • Check-in and explore Gion District – Traditional geisha area

April 21 - Kyoto Temples

  • Fushimi Inari Shrine – Thousands of red torii gates
  • Kiyomizu-dera – Temple with stunning hillside views

April 22 - Arashiyama

  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • Togetsukyo Bridge and Monkey Park

April 23 - Nara Day Trip

  • Nara Deer Park
  • Todai-ji Temple – Giant Buddha statue

April 24 - Osaka Arrival

  • Travel from Kyoto to Osaka
  • Explore Dotonbori – Vibrant nightlife and food

April 25 - Osaka Castle & Kuromon Market

  • Osaka Castle – Historical landmark with museum
  • Kuromon Ichiba Market – Street food & local eats

April 26 - Day Trip to Himeji

  • Himeji Castle – Japan’s most beautiful original castle
  • Koko-en Garden – Traditional Japanese gardens nearby

April 27 - Shopping & Leisure in Osaka

  • Namba – Shopping and entertainment hub
  • Optional: Spa World or day spa

April 28 - Universal Studios Japan

  • Full day at Universal Studios
  • Experience rides, attractions, and themed worlds

April 29 - Osaka Castle Revisit & Dotonbori

  • Morning: Osaka Castle garden walk
  • Explore Dotonbori & Don Quijote
  • Evening: Night views and river walk

April 30 - Return to Delhi

  • Flight from Kansai Airport to HKG (2hr layover)
  • Flight from HKG to Delhi
