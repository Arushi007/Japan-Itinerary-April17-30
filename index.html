Japan Itinerary: April 18-19

Explore Tokyo, Mt. Fuji, and more! Discover the beauty and culture of Japan.

April 18: Tokyo Exploration

Arrival: 17:00 at Sequence Iyashita Hotel

Activities: Visit Meiji Shrine, Yoyogi Park, Asakusa Sensoji Temple, Tokyo Tower, and Takeshita Street.

Start the day with a peaceful visit to the Meiji Shrine, then explore the bustling Yoyogi Park, followed by the famous Asakusa Sensoji Temple.

Evening: Shibuya Crossing & Ginza

Explore the vibrant Shibuya Crossing, try delicious street food, and shop in the upscale Ginza district.

End your evening with a delicious dinner at one of the many food stalls near Shibuya, then head to Ginza for an upscale shopping experience.

April 19: Mt. Fuji & Oshino Hakkai Adventure

Departure from Tokyo: 07:15 AM from Shinjuku Center Building

Activities: Seasonal fruit picking, flower viewing at various parks, Mt. Fuji 5th Station, Kachi-Kachi Ropeway, Oshino Hakkai.

Start the day with a scenic train ride to Yamanashi, followed by fruit picking and breathtaking views of Mt. Fuji. Enjoy a Mt. Fuji special lunch box at the 5th Station before taking the Kachi-Kachi Ropeway for panoramic views.

Oshino Hakkai & Return to Tokyo

Explore the scenic Oshino Hakkai, then return to Tokyo.

After visiting Oshino Hakkai, head back to Tokyo, reflecting on your beautiful day surrounded by nature and culture.
