Japan Travel Itinerary

April 17 - Arrival in Tokyo

  • 10:00 AM: Arrive in Tokyo, Japan. After checking into your hotel, spend the rest of the day acclimating and exploring the local neighborhood.
  • 02:00 PM: Head out for a quick tour of the iconic Shibuya Crossing and enjoy shopping in the surrounding area.
  • 06:00 PM: Dinner at a traditional Japanese restaurant in Shibuya, serving sushi and sashimi.

April 18 - Exploring Tokyo: Asakusa and Odaiba

  • 09:00 AM: Visit the famous Asakusa Temple and explore the Nakamise shopping street. Don’t forget to try some traditional Japanese snacks!
  • 12:00 PM: Head to Odaiba for a futuristic shopping experience and sightseeing at teamLab Borderless, a digital art museum.
  • 06:00 PM: Dinner at Odaiba’s waterfront, overlooking the Rainbow Bridge.

April 19 - Mt. Fuji

  • 09:00 AM: Take a day trip to Mt. Fuji. Start your journey by visiting the Fuji Five Lakes area and enjoy stunning views of the mountain.
  • 12:00 PM: Visit the Chureito Pagoda for a panoramic view of Mt. Fuji. Don’t forget your camera!
  • 03:00 PM: Explore the nearby hot springs and relax in the natural mineral baths.

April 20 - Mt. Fuji and Surrounding Area

  • 09:00 AM: Take a hike around the foothills of Mt. Fuji and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding nature.
  • 12:00 PM: Visit the Oshino Hakkai village, a UNESCO World Heritage site with beautiful ponds and old traditional houses.
  • 03:00 PM: Head back to Tokyo in the evening, preparing for the next day's adventure at Tokyo Disneyland!

April 21 - Tokyo Disneyland

  • 09:00 AM: **Arrival at Tokyo Disneyland** - Start your day at one of the most iconic theme parks in the world. Spend the day experiencing the magic of Disney, from meeting beloved characters to enjoying thrilling rides.
  • 12:00 PM: **Lunch at Disneyland** - Enjoy a variety of Disney-themed meals and snacks. Don’t miss out on classic Disney park food!
  • 03:00 PM: **Explore Fantasyland and Tomorrowland** - Take time to explore the themed areas of the park.
  • 06:00 PM: **Disney Parade and Fireworks** - Watch the spectacular Disney parade followed by an enchanting fireworks display.

April 22 - Wisteria Ashikaga Flower Park

  • 09:00 AM: **Arrive at Ashikaga Flower Park** - Explore the stunning Wisteria Ashikaga Flower Park, famous for its magnificent wisteria blossoms in full bloom.
  • 11:00 AM: **Wisteria Tunnel** - Walk through the beautiful wisteria tunnel, a captivating sight with hanging flowers.
  • 12:30 PM: **Lunch at the Park** - Enjoy lunch at the park's restaurant while admiring the surrounding flowers.
  • 02:00 PM: **Explore the Park** - Visit other flower displays and enjoy the picturesque scenery of the park.

April 23 - Kyoto - Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine

  • 09:00 AM: Travel to Kyoto and head to the Fushimi Inari Shrine, famous for its endless rows of red torii gates.
  • 12:00 PM: Climb the mountain to reach the top, offering scenic views of the city and surrounding areas.
  • 03:00 PM: Explore the shrine and its smaller temples. You can also purchase souvenirs from local vendors.

April 24 - Kyoto - Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)

  • 09:00 AM: Visit the iconic Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) in Kyoto, a Zen Buddhist temple covered in gold leaf and surrounded by beautiful gardens and ponds.
  • 12:00 PM: Enjoy a peaceful walk around the grounds, taking in the serene atmosphere and stunning architecture.
  • 03:00 PM: Have lunch at a nearby restaurant offering traditional Kyoto-style cuisine.

April 25 - Kyoto - Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

  • 09:00 AM: Head to Arashiyama and explore the famous Bamboo Grove, a serene forest of tall bamboo stalks that create a tranquil, almost otherworldly atmosphere.
  • 12:00 PM: Take a walk along the river or visit the nearby Tenryu-ji Temple.
  • 03:00 PM: Enjoy some quiet time in the grove, then have lunch at a nearby restaurant.

April 26 - Nara Day Trip

  • 09:00 AM: Take a day trip to Nara, known for its friendly deer and beautiful temples.
  • 11:00 AM: Visit Todai-ji Temple, home to the largest bronze Buddha statue in Japan.
  • 01:00 PM: Spend time exploring Nara Park, feeding the deer and enjoying the serene environment.
  • 03:00 PM: Visit Kasuga Taisha Shrine, a beautiful and tranquil location with hundreds of stone lanterns.

April 27 - Osaka

  • 09:00 AM: Visit Osaka Castle, one of Japan's most famous landmarks, known for its impressive architecture and beautiful surrounding park.
  • 12:00 PM: Explore the nearby Osaka Museum of History for a deeper understanding of the city's culture.
  • 03:00 PM: Stroll through the bustling Dotonbori district and enjoy some street food, such as takoyaki and okonomiyaki.

April 28 - Osaka - Universal Studios Japan

  • 09:00 AM: Spend the day at Universal Studios Japan, experiencing exciting attractions like Harry Potter World and Jurassic Park.
  • 12:00 PM: Have lunch at one of the themed restaurants within the park.
  • 06:00 PM: End your day with a night parade and the park's spectacular evening show.

April 29 - Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park

  • 09:00 AM: Visit the Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park, dedicated to the victims of the atomic bombing in 1945. Explore the Peace Memorial Museum and the iconic Atomic Bomb Dome.
  • 12:00 PM: Pay your respects at the Cenotaph for the A-bomb victims.
  • 03:00 PM: Spend time reflecting at the Peace Memorial Park and visit the surrounding gardens.

April 30 - Departure

  • 09:00 AM: After breakfast, head to the airport for your flight back home.
