Japan Travel Itinerary

April 17 - Flight from Delhi to Haneda Airport

  • 🛫 10:00 AM: Depart from Indira Gandhi International Airport
  • 🛬 10:00 PM: Arrive at Haneda Airport, Tokyo
  • 🚗 Transfer to hotel

April 18 - Asakusa, Sensoji Temple, Ueno, Shibuya

  • 🛕 Visit Sensoji Temple, Tokyo's oldest temple
  • 🌳 Explore Ueno Park and its museums
  • 🌆 Experience the Shibuya Crossing and Hachiko Statue

April 19 - Mount Fuji Day Trip

  • 🗻 Travel to Kawaguchiko area for Mount Fuji views
  • 🚠 Ride the Mount Fuji Panoramic Ropeway
  • 🛍️ Visit Fuji-Q Highland or Lake Kawaguchi

April 20 - Kyoto: Fushimi Inari & Gion

  • 🚄 Take a bullet train to Kyoto
  • ⛩️ Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine with its thousands of Torii gates
  • 🎎 Explore Gion District in the evening

April 21 - Arashiyama Bamboo Forest & Monkey Park

  • 🎍 Walk through Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • 🐒 Visit Iwatayama Monkey Park
  • 🛶 Optional boat ride on the Hozugawa River

April 22 - Nara Day Trip

  • 🦌 Feed the deer in Nara Park
  • ⛩️ Visit Todai-ji Temple with the Great Buddha
  • 🌸 Relax in Isuien Garden

April 23 - Kyoto to Osaka

  • 🚅 Take train to Osaka
  • 🌇 Explore Dotonbori, famous for neon lights & food
  • 🍜 Try local delicacies like takoyaki and okonomiyaki

April 24 - Osaka: Aquarium & Tempozan Ferris Wheel

  • 🐠 Visit Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan
  • 🎡 Ride the Tempozan Ferris Wheel
  • 🛍️ Explore shopping at Tempozan Marketplace

April 25 - Osaka to Hiroshima & Miyajima

  • 🚄 Bullet train to Hiroshima
  • ☮️ Visit Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park
  • ⛩️ Ferry to Miyajima Island, see Itsukushima Shrine

April 26 - Return to Osaka

  • 🚄 Return by train from Hiroshima
  • 🌆 Free evening in Osaka

April 27 - Day trip to Kobe

  • 🚆 Visit Kobe Harborland
  • 🥩 Try Kobe beef
  • 🧀 Explore the ropeway or Nunobiki Herb Garden

April 28 - Namba and Shopping

  • 🛍️ Spend the day shopping in Namba and Shinsaibashi
  • 🎭 Enjoy street performances and food stalls

April 29 - Osaka Castle & Universal Studios

  • 🏯 Visit Osaka Castle
  • 🎢 Head to Universal Studios Japan
  • 🍜 Dinner and walk at Dotonbori Market

April 30 - Return to Delhi

  • ✈️ Fly from Kansai International Airport to Hong Kong
  • 🕐 2-hour layover in Hong Kong
  • ✈️ Continue flight to Delhi
