Japan Travel Itinerary | April 18 - 20

April 18 - Tokyo Arrival & Hotel Check-in

Arrive at Haneda Airport and check in to the Shinjuku Granbell Hotel.

April 19 - Explore Asakusa & Shibuya

Visit Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa and cross the famous Shibuya Crossing.

April 20 - Mount Fuji Day Trip

Take a scenic trip to Mount Fuji and Lake Kawaguchi.
