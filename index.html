Japan Travel Itinerary: April 18-22, 2025

April 18, 2025 - Arrival in Tokyo

  • 10:00 AM - Arrive at Tokyo International Airport
  • Transfer to Hotel for Check-in
  • Explore Local Cuisine for Lunch
  • Afternoon Shopping at Ginza
  • 6:00 PM - Dinner at Tokyo Tower

April 19, 2025 - Wisteria Story & Butterfly Sea Day Tour

  • 10:00 AM - Departure from Tokyo
  • 12:00 PM - Visit Hitachi Seaside Park

  • 3:00 PM - Visit Kamiiso-no-Torii: Famous seaside torii gates
  • 5:30 PM - Visit Ashikaga Flower Park: Known for its beautiful wisteria flowers, part of the 2025 Wisteria Story Grand Festival

April 22, 2025 - Wisteria Story Tour at Ashikaga Flower Park

  • 10:00 AM - Departure for Ashikaga Flower Park
  • 12:00 PM - Explore the Wisteria Flowers: Ashikaga is famous for its dreamy wisteria flowers, like the "soul tree" in Avatar.
  • 3:00 PM - Evening Lighting: Witness the enchanting lights that make the wisteria look even more magical at night.
  • 5:00 PM - Take Photos: Capture the stunning sights of the glowing wisteria flowers
  • 8:00 PM - Return to Tokyo

  • 1:10 PM - Explore Owakudani
