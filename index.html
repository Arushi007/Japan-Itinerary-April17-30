Japan Travel Itinerary | April 18-20

April 18: Tokyo Exploration

Arrival: Sequence Iyashita Hotel - 17:00

Activities:

  • Meiji Shrine (17:30–19:00) - Visit the iconic shrine surrounded by lush forest.
  • Yoyogi Park Stroll (19:00–19:30) - Enjoy a relaxing walk in the tranquil park.
  • Asakusa Sensoji Temple (19:45–20:30) - Explore one of Tokyo's oldest and most famous temples.
  • Takeshita Street Shopping (21:45–22:30) - Wander through a trendy street filled with boutiques and food stalls.

April 19: Mt. Fuji & Oshino Hakkai Adventure

Departure: 07:15 AM from Shinjuku

Activities:

  • Fruit Picking & Flower Viewing (08:30–10:00) - Experience picking fresh fruits and admiring beautiful flowers.
  • Mt. Fuji 5th Station (10:30–12:00) - Take in the breathtaking views from the 5th station of Mt. Fuji.
  • Kachi-Kachi Ropeway (12:30–13:00) - Ride the ropeway for panoramic views of Lake Kawaguchi and Mt. Fuji.
  • Oshino Hakkai (14:30–16:00) - Explore the famous natural springs with stunning views of Mt. Fuji.

April 20: Mt. Fuji & Hakone Day Tour

Departure: 08:00 AM from Shinjuku

Activities:

  • Hakone Shrine & Lake Ashi’s Sea Torii Gate (10:30–12:00) - Visit the serene shrine and the famous Torii gate by the lake.
  • Hakone Pirate Ship (12:10–12:40) - Cruise around Lake Ashi on the unique pirate ship.
  • Owakudani (13:10–14:00) - Explore the volcanic valley and enjoy views of Mt. Fuji.
  • Lake Yamanaka (15:00–15:30) - Relax by the picturesque lake and enjoy views of Mount Fuji.
  • Oshino Hakkai (15:40–16:20) - Visit the iconic Oshino Hakkai with crystal clear ponds and views of Mt. Fuji.
